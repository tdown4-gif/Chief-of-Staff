# Founder Notes

## Core Insight

People do not need another place to organize their lives. They need a system that absorbs context and gives it back when useful.

The user should never have to be their own librarian.

## Product Feel

Chief of Staff should feel:

- Quiet.
- Competent.
- Specific.
- Trustworthy.
- Useful without ceremony.
- Better after every capture.

## What We Are Building

A proactive AI chief-of-staff and memory system that can:

- Capture anything.
- Organize automatically.
- Build context.
- Remember over time.
- Research proactively.
- Suggest actions.

## What We Are Not Building In V1

- Full CRM
- Full calendar app
- Travel booking
- Password manager
- Expense management suite
- Enterprise collaboration
- Browser extension
- Native mobile app
- Complex dashboards

## Important Product Tension

The product must be powerful enough to feel like a chief of staff, but constrained enough to ship a small useful version.

The first version should prove memory and proactive usefulness, not breadth.

## Future Vision

- Travel preference learning
- Gift reminders
- Resume updates
- Skills graph
- Opportunity detection
- SBIR tracking
- Relationship intelligence
- AI-generated project creation
