# Software Landscape

## Purpose

This file tracks software and product categories adjacent to Chief of Staff.

The goal is not to copy these products. The goal is to understand where the market is crowded, where incumbents are strong, and where a founder/operator memory layer can still be differentiated.

## AI Executive Assistants And Workflow Agents

- Lindy: AI executive assistant for inbox, calendar, meeting prep, follow-ups, and workflow automation.
- Motion / UseMotion: AI productivity platform for calendar, task planning, projects, docs, and scheduling.
- Reclaim.ai: AI calendar/time-management tool for focus time, habits, tasks, meetings, and scheduling.
- Akiflow: Time-management command center with calendar, tasks, routines, integrations, and AI assistance.

## AI Memory, Second Brain, And Personal Knowledge

- Mem: AI memory/thought-partner app for brain dumps, notes, meetings, research, and automatic organization.
- Granola: AI meeting-notes and meeting-memory product.
- Rewind: Personal memory/lifelogging product focused on capturing user activity.
- Limitless: Ambient memory/lifelogging and wearable memory product.
- Obsidian: Local-first personal knowledge base using Markdown, backlinks, plugins, and graph view.
- Tana: AI-native knowledge workspace with structured notes, graph-style organization, and workflows.
- Capacities: Object-based personal knowledge management app.

## Strategic Read

The market is crowded around capture, notes, tasks, calendars, meetings, automation, and generic AI assistance.

Chief of Staff should not try to beat every product in its own category.

The most defensible wedge is trusted follow-through memory for high-context professionals:

- Capture messy context.
- Extract commitments, people, projects, dates, reminders, and durable memory.
- Preserve provenance.
- Ask for approval before external action.
- Surface timely briefings and suggested next actions.

The product should win by knowing what matters and helping the user follow through, not by becoming another all-in-one productivity suite.
